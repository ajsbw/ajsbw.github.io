Civil and finishing is our forte!!

The AJS team can finish any building up to the smallest detail - right from the structure, insulation, ceiling, plastering, painting, flooring, glass works, decorative plaster, painting, wallpapers, etc. We also undertake barbending, concrete works, block work, pavers, kerb stone, excavation and concrete road works. Some of our most sought-after works are outlined below :

  • Structure
  • Painting
  • Doors & Windows
  • Tiles, Granites & Marble Laying
  • Railing Works
  • Signages & Floor Marking
  • Structural Steel Works
  • Barbending

In addition, we also cater to all types of railin work and MS structural works.

Trust us and we will make sure to provide you the expected ambience both indoor and outdoor. Our team of architects, designers and executors works in complete sync so that when the project is finished you will be more than pleased with the final result.

Our strong belief is that there is lot of charm in the minutest details and that is why we strive to give our clients the highest quality and beauty – right from the conceptual stage of the project uptil the fully finished design.

We provide innovative solutions for sustainable progress.
